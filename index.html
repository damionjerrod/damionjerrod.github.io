Who is Damion Jerrod?

I've never been someone who enjoys being in the spotlight. I'm an introvert by nature so it's natural for me to lean towards solidarity. Unfortunately though, life isn't as understanding. The older I get, the more I realize we're all created to be connected. We exist for each other-in a weird way-and the more we resist our innate affinity towards compassion we begin to wither. I'm just a guy who's decided to embrace that-to embrace compassion and in turn, live out loud.
